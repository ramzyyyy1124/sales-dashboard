Tableau Sales Analysis Dashboard
 Project Overview
  This project analyzes sales data to uncover trends, top-performing products, and regional performance.
Tools Used
 Tableau Public
 Excel
 Key Insights
  Copiers and Phones are the top profit drivers, contributing the highest profits among all sub-categories. 
  Tables is the biggest loss-making sub-category, indicating pricing, cost, or discount issues.
  Bookcases and Supplies also show negative or low profit, suggesting a need for cost control or strategy change.
  A small set of top products drives a large share of revenue, highlighting product concentration.
  Sales trend shows consistent growth over time, with periodic peaks indicating seasonal demand.
  High sales do not always mean high profit, emphasizing the importance of profit-focused analysis.
